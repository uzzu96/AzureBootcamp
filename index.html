About this Event

The Global Azure Bootcamp is an event conducted by Microsoft annually across the globe and its happening on the 21st of April. What's more exciting is that its coming to BITS Pilani, Dubai Campus in Dubai International Academic City. This is your chance to learn the skills and tools to scale up your current and future businesses.

You will get an opportunity to learn about one of the most powerful cloud platform by Microsoft, network with fellow professionals and students, and collaborate on exciting projects on the Cloud.
